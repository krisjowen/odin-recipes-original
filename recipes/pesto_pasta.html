Pesto Pasta

Description

As far as quick comfort food goes a bowl of pesto pasta is hard to beat.

This recipe is as simple as two main ingredients and can be ready in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Any pasta
  • A jar of pesto

Steps

  1. Bring a pan of salted water to the boil.
  2. Once bubbling heavily, add the pasta and cook for as long as the packet instructions.
  3. When ready, drain the pasta and return to the pan.
  4. Stir in the jar of pesto before dividing between two warmed bowl.
