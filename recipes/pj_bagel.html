PJ Bagel

Description

A classic combination, this recipe is for a peanut butter and jelly bagel.

There are two types of people - those who love peanut butter and jelly together and those who haven't tried it yet.

Ingredients

  • Bagels
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Slice the bagel in two lengthways.
  2. Toast the bagel.
  3. Spread peanut butter on one half of the toasted bagel and jam on the other.
  4. Assemble like a sandwich.
