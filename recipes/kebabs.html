Kebabs

Description

This is a recipe for vegetarian kebabs made with halloumi and vegetables.

It is an ideal recipe to make with children and is bound to please veggies and meat eaters alike!

Ingredients

  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Halloumi
  • Hoummous
  • Flatbread

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a frying pan.
  2. Add the peppers and onions and fry until softened.
  3. Add the halloumi to the frying pan.
  4. Whilst everything cooks, put the flatbreads in a toaster.
  5. When the flatbreads are ready, spread some hoummous and cover with the rest of the ingredients.
